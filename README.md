### Relevant flags

N/A = undefined behavior, sp = space.

| flags | -   | .   | 0   | sp  | +   | #            |
| ----- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | ------------ |
| di    | Y   | Y   | Y   | Y   | Y   | N/A          |
| u     | Y   | Y   | Y   | N/A | N/A | N/A          |
| xX    | Y   | Y   | Y   | N/A | N/A | prefix 0x/0X |
| p     | Y   | N/A | N/A | N/A | N/A | N/A          |
| cs    | Y   | Y   | N/A | N/A | N/A | N/A          |

- '-' overrides '0' if both are used.
- '+' overrides ' ' if both are used.
